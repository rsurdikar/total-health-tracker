Total Health Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Data The App Handles

Total Health Tracker stores the following data locally on the user's device:

  • Profile details entered by the user: weight, height, age, gender, and activity level.
  • Food logs: meal names, calories, timestamps, and meal categories.
  • Walk summaries: distance, duration, active calories, start time, and end time.
  • Meal-plan and exercise-plan preferences generated or selected in the app.

The app uses location permission to track walks when the user starts walk tracking. Location is used to calculate distance and walk metrics.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Google may process device identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, and other advertising signals in accordance with Google's advertising policies and the user's consent choices.

Storage And Sharing

Health, nutrition, and walking data are stored locally on the device. The app does not currently provide account sync or export to a backend service.

Google AdMob may process advertising-related data. See Google's privacy policy for details.

User Control

Users can deny location permission, though walk tracking will not work without it. Users can delete app data through Android system settings by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Health Disclaimer

Calorie, BMR, TDEE, walk-calorie, meal-plan, and weight-goal values are estimates for personal tracking. They are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should consult a qualified clinician before major diet changes, very low calorie plans, pregnancy-related goals, eating-disorder recovery, or medication-supported weight loss.

Your Consent

By using the app, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this privacy policy.
